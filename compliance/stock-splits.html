Stock Split Policy

Payment Day Procedures

We freeze all asset activity the day before a split becomes effective at 6PM Eastern (22:00:00 UTC). During the next two hours, we effect the split. We unfreeze accounts the day the split is effective beginning at 00:00:00 UTC (not to exceed 2:00:00 UTC) or 8PM Eastern (10PM Eastern). As such, there exist at least two hours on the day before a split is 'effective' in New York time during which investors can transfer their post-split balances.

Rule 17Ad-15 Statement

We find these operations in accordance with Rule 17Ad-15 since, inter alia, the account freeze in question (i) does not last longer than three business days, (ii) affects all investors essentially equally, and (iii) notifies investors immediately of transfer requests rejected because of frozen split processing.
